Our Story

A Concept by
Cube 65

Nestled in the heart of Victoria Island, GRÄS is an upscale all-day dining destination that masterfully blends flavors from around the globe. From the moment you step off the elevator, you're greeted by a vintage Rolls-Royce in the lobby and an opulent atmosphere that defines luxury dining in Lagos.

Our menu is a fusion of African, Italian, European, and Mediterranean cuisines — expertly crafted by our culinary team using premium imported ingredients, including Black Angus Australian beef and the freshest Norwegian salmon.

Beyond Dining

The Experience

GRÄS is more than a restaurant — it's a destination for those who appreciate the finer things in life

Fine Dining

World-class cuisine with exquisite plating. Black Angus beef, Norwegian salmon, and handmade pasta crafted to perfection.

Craft Cocktails

Signature cocktails like the Adriatic Sea — rum, tequila, strawberry, coconut & blackberry served in artful glassware.

Shisha Lounge

Unwind in our outdoor shisha lounge with premium flavors, comfortable seating, and a perfect evening atmosphere.

Live Entertainment

Enjoy beautiful live violin performances creating the perfect ambiance for special occasions and romantic evenings.

Private Events

Host corporate gatherings, celebrations, and intimate dinners in our elegantly designed space with personalized service.

All-Day Dining

Open from 12 PM to midnight, GRÄS is perfect for power lunches, afternoon cocktails, romantic dinners, and late-night indulgence.
